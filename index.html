HOW TO PLAY

Objective: Be the first player to get all your tokens to the center of the board.

Gameplay:

  • Roll the dice on your turn
  • Move one of your tokens according to the dice roll
  • Landing on an opponent's token sends it back to the starting area
  • First player to get all tokens home wins the cash prize!
